Return 2,000 years to the times just preceding the manifestation of Christ in the world. The Time Spirit is abroad in the hearts and minds of people, just as it also was 4,000 years earlier when the first revelations of the Tree of Life There are footpaths leading everywhere across the continent, (almost all of them have been covered over by today's interstate roads). These pathways lead all throughout the great forests of the East, from Canada and the Great Lakes and across the magnificently sculptured deserts of the WestThere are gatherings at sacred sites
