A GREAT CHAIN

(And the meaning of "A Thousand Years")

 

And I saw an angel (Divine Reality) come down from heaven, having the key of the bottomless pit (the very depths of the consciousness of that Reality itself), and A GREAT CHAIN in his hand.

And he laid hold on the Dragon (the spirit of antichrist in the earth...Ezekiel 29:1-3), that old Serpent, which is the Devil, and Satan,and bound him a Thousand years...

As there was 1,000 years between the time of David and the time of Christ, so there has been a figurative period of 1,000 years from the time that the judgments wars of the 20th century began to fall upon the City of David and the manifestation of the Messiahs again in our time...(Psalm 90:4-12; Isaiah 29; 2 Peter 3:3-12).
10 X 10 X 10 (The cube of the mystery of the Tree of Life) = 1,000...(Revelation 21:10-16).

And cast him into the bottomless pit, and shut him up, and set a seal upon him, that he should deceive the nations no more, till the Thousand years years should be fulfilled, and after that he must be loosed a little season.

And I saw thrones, and they that sat upon them, and judgment was given unto them, and I saw the souls of them that were beheaded for the witness of Jesus, and for the word of God, and which had not worshipped the Beast, neither his image, neither had received his mark upon their foreheads, or in their hands (Deuteronomy 6:1-9; Revelation 13:16-18); and they lived and reigned with Christ a Thousand years...

Do you not know that the Head of Prophecy was cut off with John (and, of course, Paul)?

I said, Lord can it be possible to remove the Head of Prophecy?

He said, When you come to know what "head" means, and prophecy issues from the head, then understand the meaning of "the head was removed."...(The Apocryphon of James; Psalm 74:5-9).

But the rest of the dead lived not again until the Thousand years were finished. THIS IS THE FIRST RESURRECTION.

Blessed and holy is he that hath part in the First Resurrection: on such the second death (the death of the body itself) hath no power, but they shall be as priests of God and of Christ, and shall reign with him a Thousand years.

And when the Thousand years are expired, Satan shall be loosed out of his prison.

And shall go out to deceive the nations which are in the four quarters of the earth, Gog and Magog, to gather them together to battle...(Revelation 20:1-15).

Or else how can one enter a strong man's house and spoil his goods, except he first bind the strong man...(Matthew 12:29-30).
