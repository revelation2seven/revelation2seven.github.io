The Fullness of the Gentiles

 

Romans 15:8-12:

Now I say that Jesus Christ was a minister of the circumcision for the truth of God, to confirm the promises made unto the fathers;

And that the Gentiles might glorify God for His mercy; as it is written: For this cause will I confess to thee among the Gentiles, and sing unto thy Name. (2 Samuel 22:50; Psalm 18:49)...

And again he saith, Rejoice ye Gentiles, with His people. (Deut.32:43)...

And again, Praise the Lord all ye Gentiles; and laud Him all ye people. (Psalm 117:1)...

And again, Esaias saith, There shall be a root of Jesse, and he that shall reign over the Gentiles; in him shall the Gentiles trust. (Isaiah 11:10)...

 

And the Gentiles shall come to thy Light, and kings to the Brightness of Thy Rising...

And the abundance of the sea shall be converted unto thee, the forces of the Gentiles shall come unto thee...(Isaiah 60:1-6).

I am sought of them that asked not for me; I am found of them that sought me not; I said, Behold me, behold me, unto a nation that was not called by my Name...(Isaiah 65:1).

 

And if a stranger shall sojourn among you, AND WILL KEEP THE PASSOVER UNTO THE LORD; according to the ordinance of the Passover, and according to the manner thereof, so shall he do: YE SHALL HAVE ONE ORDINANCE, BOTH FOR THE STRANGER, AND FOR HIM THAT IS BORN IN THE LAND...(Numbers 9:14).

For there is no respect of persons with God.

For as many as have sinned without law shall also perish without law: and as many as have sinned in the law shall be judged by the law;

For not the hearers of the law are just before God, but the doers of the law shall be justified.

For when the Gentiles, which have not the law, do by nature the things contained in the law, these, having not the law, are a law unto themselves;

Which shew the work of the law written in their hearts, their conscience also bearing witness, and their thoughts the meanwhile accusing or excusing one another;

In the day that God shall judge the secrets of men by Jesus Christ according to my gospel...(Romans 2:11-16).
