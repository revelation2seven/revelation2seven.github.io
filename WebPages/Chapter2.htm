Or the story of Prometheus bound hand and foot to a "rock." All of this symbolism can be divined from the Oracle, from the Universal Mystery of Christ on the World Tree. And why was he bound? For bringing Fire to man, that is, for the sins of Adam--for making man to be as gods. (Genesis 3:22-24). This Fire, which comes to earth from heaven, from the Sun, and which burns eternally on the altar in the midst of the Temple of God, symbolizes Divine Wisdom. This knowledge of heavenly things passes from God to humanity, just as one flame is ignited by another. But when Prometheus (along with his brother, Epimetheus, Forethought and Afterthought, who together represent the beginning and the end of the entire epic), passed this Fire indiscriminately on to those who were not yet mentally and spiritually prepared to use it--evil ensued, and the 20th (and now the 21st) century is the great burning effect of it all:

"Prometheus, son of Iapetus, surpassing all in cunning, you are glad you have outwitted me and stolen Fire--a great plague to you yourself and to men that shall be. But I will give men as a price for Fire an evil in which they may be glad of heart when they embrace their own destruction." Hesiod's Works and Days.

So the elements of the entire epic, the fall and redemption, can be seen in the story of Prometheus. But for what reason would Zeus, the Earth-Shaker, Thunderbolt-Thrower (Job 37:5; 1 Samuel 2:10; Psalm 77:18-20; Haggai 2:6,7; Hebrews 12:25-29; Revelation 15:1-4) bind him to a rock in the very farthest reaches of the Pontus, unless it was to both conceal and reveal the fact that the farthest reaches of the Western Ocean (the Atlantic) were meant? Especially since his other brother Atlas was also fated to bear upon the family shoulders the entire burden of the Western world. What are these burdens but the cares of this world (along with the deceitfulness of riches). All of this is the story of Adam, whose fallen sons and daughters gave birth to an age of Giants, or Titans (Genesis 6:1-5), whose stories are all mixed together with the Hebrew story, all of whom are sitting on the thrones of empires and nations, and city-states at this very day.
