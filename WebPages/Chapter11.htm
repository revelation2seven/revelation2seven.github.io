The prophets, likewise, employed the number to symbolize things that would come to pass in the last days, in the realm of Babylon again, when the great circle of time completed itself here in the Western ends of the earth.

The number 60, a measure of Time.

The image that was set up was that of the king of Babylon himself, and in our times it is that of the Office of the Presidency of the United States of America. (Daniel, chapter 4). The image was already set up in the hearts and minds of the American people, but in Wilson it was set up in a new and greater way. It was set up in the minds of the people of the world. It is impossible not to be influenced by this man and his office, for he has MAGNIFIED himself in the earth. On the surface of every present political and military reality he is the most powerful man on the face of the earth; and in the name of Christ he, along with all his advisors and teachers, his priests and his prophets, teaches his subjects to disobey Christ. This is the Mystery of Iniquity fulfilled. (See 2 Thess. 2:7,8). The Holy Spirit strives peacefully in the hearts of humanity, calling them to come enter the Covenant of Life. Of course, into the spiritual wilderness, out from among them, as the Spirit commands. And even then one cannot find a place to follow the imperatives of the spiritual life, in the midst of such a world system that has also invaded the wilderness...
