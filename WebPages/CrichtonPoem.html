The Sea of Nun is spiritually married to the Christ
Male and female are they both in the Spirit of God
The Sacred Heart of Mary bleeds at the division and the pain of betrayal through ignorance.
For is it not shown that the thorns in the heart of Mary are the same as the thorns in the head of Christ?
When both head and heart are united again, peace and unity will reign through Love
Those who feed division are servants of darkness and not of light for the word diabolical means divided.
If we do not know this then we can all unwittingly become servants of darkness
We cannot save them, they must come to the realisation themselves
Gently, gently, with love and respect and without threat, we give the message for the listener to do what they will

Peace Unity and Love

Crichton
